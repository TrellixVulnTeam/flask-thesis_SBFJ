3 Major Steps of the Algorithm:

1. Extract - the document is extracted and an excerpt of it will be used in creating the summary.
2. Preprocess - the excerpt will be preprocessed by means of title extraction, sentence segmentation, tokenization, stop words removal, special characters removal, POS tagging and lemmatization.
3. Score - the preprocessed sentences will be scored by the following features: TFISF, Sentence Position, Numerical Token, Sentence Length, Title Overlap, Proper Noun. The sentences feature scores will be weighted and sum up to determine its final sentence score. Top scoring sentences will be used to generate the abstract
